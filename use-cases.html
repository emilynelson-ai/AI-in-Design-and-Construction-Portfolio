Page Strategy

How to Present Each Project

A simple structure helps readers understand each artifact quickly. This is especially helpful when faculty or classmates are scanning multiple projects in one sitting.

Recommended Project Elements

  • Project title
  • Short description of the assignment
  • Tool, method, or AI concept used
  • Screenshot, diagram, or other visual
  • Why the project is helpful or relevant

What It Is

Briefly name and describe the assignment or project artifact in 1–2 sentences.

How AI Was Used

Explain whether AI supported analysis, automation, ideation, visualization, or another task.

Why It Matters

Connect the work to design quality, efficiency, communication, risk reduction, or other AEC values.

Reflection

Share what you learned, what worked well, and what questions remain for future exploration.
