Weekly AI News

This page organizes weekly reviews of AI news and emerging developments. It is designed to show what sources I followed, how I summarized each story, and how I connected those developments to design, construction, and the broader AEC industry.

Source Tracking

Keep a consistent record of articles, podcasts, newsletters, or videos you reviewed each week.

Critical Reflection

Show your own analysis, not just summaries, by explaining why each topic matters and what questions it raises.

Page Structure

How to Organize Each Weekly Post

A consistent format makes your analysis easier to read and helps the site feel polished. You can repeat the same card structure each week throughout the semester.

Recommended Components

  • Source title, link, publication, and date
  • Brief summary of the main idea
  • Your reflection or critique
  • A note on relevance to AEC
  • Optional media, diagram, or embedded content

Presentation Tips

How to Keep This Page Strong

The strongest news pages combine consistency, critical thinking, and clear relevance to the course theme.

Best Practices

  • Write in your own voice and avoid sounding purely descriptive
  • Use headings and short sections for readability
  • Keep a similar structure from week to week
  • Connect general AI news back to AEC whenever possible

Optional Enhancements

  • Add thumbnails or screenshots from each source
  • Embed videos, iframes, or data visuals
  • Create filters by topic, week, or theme
  • Link each entry to a dedicated post page later
