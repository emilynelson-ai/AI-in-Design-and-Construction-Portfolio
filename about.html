About Me

As a Construction Contract Administrator for LS3P, I focus on collaborating closely with all project stakeholders to advocate for project goals and deliver successful, well-executed projects. With nearly a decade of experience spanning from the Carolinas back to my home state of Colorado, my interdisciplinary background bridges the gaps between clients, designers, and contractors.

I strive to bring a balanced perspective to every project while fostering collaborative environments where complex projects, and the people behind them, can succeed.

Current Role

In construction administration, I represent the design team during construction and serve as a primary point of contact for owners and contractors. My work includes reviewing RFIs, submittals, pay applications, and change order proposals, issuing architect's supplemental instructions and related contract modifications, performing site visits, and documenting project progress and compliance.
