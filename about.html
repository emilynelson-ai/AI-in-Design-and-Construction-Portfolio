About Me

With roots in the Colorado construction industry and a strong interest in higher education environments, I am excited to bring my experience in stakeholder management, project delivery, and cross-functional coordination to the University of Colorado Boulder. My background bridges the perspectives of clients, designers, and contractors, and I approach project work with a strong belief that successful outcomes are built through communication, consensus, and shared accountability.

In project leadership, I focus on defining scope clearly, building alignment across teams, and guiding projects through the realities of schedules, budgets, contracts, and construction. I am especially motivated by environments where thoughtful planning, strong teams, and long-term institutional goals come together to shape meaningful built outcomes.
